About

I am a polyglot developer who likes challenges and is never afraid of diving into deep territories to get a better understanding of the big picture.

During my career I have been involved with various frameworks and tools, and I have solved various technical and non-technical challenges ranging from debugging and reverse-engineering systems to hiring and managing a small team of developers. I am always keen to learn more every time, and I am always happy to pass on my knowledge to anyone who wishes to learn more.
